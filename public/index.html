Disc Golf is fun!

The great things about Disc Golf

  • It's cheap
  • You get outside
  • You get exercise
  • Everyone of most any age and any skill level can play

Things needed to play Disc Golf

  1. Golf Discs
  2. A bag for your discs and water.
  3. A local course, they are everywhere, and usually free.

The 22nd Street Park in Tampa is my favorite local course.It is beautiful.

"The 9th hole at 22nd Street Park is an excellent example of typical Florida Disc Golf, beautiful yes, but very low ceilings."

The Tampa Bay area has some excellent National Tournament Courses as well.
